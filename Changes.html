Convenience

With the introduction of digital cameras, photography became significantaly more convenient.
A photographer could review a photo immediately after taking it, rather than finishing an entire photo shoot, mailing the film rolls to a lab, and waiting for the developed films to be shipped back
in addition, one could change the ISO on the fly, rather to sticking to a single ISO for the duration of a shoot


Film Scanning

Many analog/film shooters may choose to digitize their film photos.
Most shops will have large flatbed scanners to do this, however, many photographers may want to scan their films themselves, but these flatbedscanners are expensive.
